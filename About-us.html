About Heriwadi Christian School

Welcome to Heriwadi Christian School

Heriwadi Christian School is a faith-based institution dedicated to providing holistic education that nurtures the mind, body, and spirit of our learners. Our mission is to develop leaders who are equipped with the skills and values to make a positive impact in the world.

OUR MISSION

  • Create an environment for holistic learning
  • Encourage learners to discover and develop leadership skills
  • Equip learners to be achievers

OUR VISION

Learners with a holistic approach to education coupled with leadership skills.

Our History

Established in 2018, Heriwadi Christian School has grown from a small community school to a leading educational institution in Ongata Rongai. Over the years, we have remained committed to our core values of excellence, holistic growth, and servant leadership.

Heriwadi Christian Schools enrolled her first student in September 2018. This was the third term for the school calendar. On that first enrolment, there was an intake of two learners. The term ended with the two learners.

The following year, HCS was determined to offer education and opened her doors for learners. There was an enrolment of three new learners, but one of the previous learners had dropped. There were four learners by the end of the first term.
